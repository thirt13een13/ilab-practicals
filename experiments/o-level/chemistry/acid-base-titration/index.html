How the titration works

  1. Choose your acid, base, and indicator in the left panel — the apparatus works the same way for any combination.
  2. Drag a Retort Stand onto the bench, then set its height.
  3. Drag a 4-Prong Clamp onto the stand and set its reach/height.
  4. Drag a Burette into the clamp's jaws.
  5. Optionally drag a Funnel onto the burette's top opening.
  6. Drag the acid beaker over the burette top — it tilts and pours the acid in.
  7. Drag the Pipette to the base beaker to draw 20/25 cc, then to a Conical Flask to dispense.
  8. Drag the Dropper to the indicator beaker, then click over the flask to add drops.
  9. Drag the flask under the burette — it snaps so the burette tip aligns with the flask's centre axis, base resting on the bench.
  10. Open the tap valve. Small openings drip; wider openings give a steady laminar stream.
  11. Swirl the flask as you go. Watch the indicator change colour at the endpoint — the colour and the volume needed depend on which acid, base, and indicator you picked.
  12. Log each trial, repeat, then read the averaged result and calculated base concentration.
